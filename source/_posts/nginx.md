# nginx

## 1.常用命令

启动：start nginx

关闭：nginx -s stop

重启：nginx -s reload

关闭所有nginx进程： 

1. windows：taskkill /f /t /im nginx.exe

2. linux: killall -9 nginx  或者 ps -A|grep nginx![image-20210910183138019](https://gitee.com/gsshy/picgo/raw/master/img/image-20210910183138019.png)
